Who Are We?

Avoice (Against Violence to Overseas Chinese Women Program) is a youth-led NGO that aims to eliminate gender-based violence, advocate for gender equality, and assist Chinese migrant women facing domestic violence overseas. Avoice was launched in 2020 by the International Network of Young Volunteers, composed of Chinese university students and overseas Chinese students. Since 2020, we have attracted over 300 volunteers from 20+ countries with diverse backgrounds. Avoice has since partnered with 70 non-profit organizations and academic institutions that are concerned about female empowerment.

We strive to help victims with a holistic approach – not only through urgently-needed approaches like psychological, financial, and legal assistance, but also through long-term approaches like research and advocacy. We strive to reduce future victims through preventative educational methods of domestic violence alleviation. We hope to give Chinese migrant women - a group often neglected in domestic violence interventions, more avenues for support.
