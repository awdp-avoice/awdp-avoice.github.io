Observations on Global Anti-Domestic Violence Policies Released During the Pandemic

March 14, 2021 in 学术研究

Following the global outbreak of the COVID-19, “inequalities” in various forms become increasingly prominent in our society, domestic violence as one existing in family lives. “Home”, for many women and children in certain countries, is likewise considered a rather dangerous place amid the pandemic. The mounting severity of domestic violence is now drawing attention from both society and international organizations.
