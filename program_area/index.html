AWDP Program Areas

Initiatives against gender-based violence

01

Vision: Collaborate with government agencies, research institutes, social organizations and media on anti-gender violence initiatives. Enhance the awareness of challenges faced by AAPI women overseas through media campaigns, legislative efforts, and call-to-action activities.

Initiatives:

  • Case Studies: Focused case studies on gender violence and gender-related challenges.

  • Region-Specific Research: Research on the state of gender violence in specific countries/regions

  • Public Awareness: Raise public awareness of the survival and development challenges faced by AAPI women through collaboration with related organizations

  • Policy and Legislative Efforts: Empirically conducting policy advocacy based on evidence-based research, and prompting overseas governments to improve protection and support systems for the special plight of Asian women

Protection over oversease Asian women

02

Vision: Establish a rescue system for overseas Asian women, intervene in violence against Asian women, and meet the general assistance needs of overseas Asian women through comprehensive assistance and referral services.

Initiatives:

  • Language Support: Multilingual Translation | Sign Language Service for the Hearing Impaired

  • Legal Support: Case Analysis and Legal Assistance | Legal Popularization Handbook

  • Psychological Support: Customized Psychological Counseling｜Psychological Healing Course

  • Shelter: emergency safe shelter | Multiple assistance services

  • Writing and speaking: Recording the experiences of victims of violence｜Sharing anti-violence relief agencies｜Practical experience and advice

  • Humanitarian aid: provide cash assistance for extreme poverty, injury, illness, unemployment, etc. caused by gender-based violence

  • Overseas aid network: Build a national aid information database | Establish a female aid cooperation network, share aid resources, and make quick aid referrals

Violence-free community

03

Vision: Create a female-friendly non-violence space, provide an international platform for overseas Asian women to communicate, express, learn, and help each other, improve their ability to prevent gender-based violence, and enable them to have a broader perspective and a more positive attitude to face their future lives

Initiatives:

  • International Community Matrix

    • Build a country-based online community of Asian women

    • Forming a matrix of Asian women’s international community

    • Organize and carry out learning and exchange activities such as gender awareness enhancement, self-protection, legal knowledge popularization, and group psychological counseling

  • Women’s Mutual Support Group

    • Establish an offline women’s mutual aid group

    • Organize local exchange activities such as reading clubs, movie viewing sessions, and sharing sessions

    • Expand the social relationships of overseas Asian women and promote peer support

  • Violence-Free Metaverse

    • Joint academic institutions and international organizations to explore gender-based violence prevention mechanisms and standards for building female-friendly virtual environments in the metaverse

    • Open up a space for Asian women to communicate in the Metaverse

Women's leadership programs

04

Vision: 目标：Cultivate the employability and leadership of overseas Asian women, strengthen employment support, give them the opportunity to show their potential, and enhance the social status and influence of overseas Asian women.

Initiatives:

  • Asian Women Global Development Forum

    • Hosting the annual Asian Women’s Global Development Forum

    • Showcasing the outstanding contributions of Asian women in the global sustainable development process

    • Demonstrate the opportunities and challenges faced by Asian women overseas

    • Share countries’ practical experience in protecting minority women and promoting gender equality

  • Joint training of women’s leadership

    • Develop a “Female Leadership Joint Training Program” with companies and institutions that focus on women’s empowerment

    • Through participatory learning methods such as workshops and seminars, cultivate key skills such as communication, reasoning, and decision-making, awaken female awareness, and break gender stereotypes and shackles

  • Overseas employment promotion

    • Provide vocational skills and vocational comprehensive quality training

    • Provide career planning consultation

    • Establish a network of women-friendly employers to create fair employment opportunities for overseas Asian women
