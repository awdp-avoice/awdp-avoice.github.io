Asian Women Development Plan

Our Work

Asian Women Development Plan·International (AWDP) was launched in the early days of the COVID-19 pandemic in 2020. As a women-centered international humanitarian development organization, AWDP is dedicated to eliminating violence and discrimination against Asian women, as well as promoting protection and development of Asian women.
