Our Work

Against Violence to Overseas Chinese Women Program (or Avoice, for short) launched in the early days of the COVID-19 pandemic in 2020. Avoice is committed to eliminating gender-based violence, promoting gender equality, and providing adequate and sustainable protection and assistance for overseas Chinese women threatened by domestic violence.
