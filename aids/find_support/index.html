Offline Supports (Belgium)

Please add the Wechat account of 'Avoice women aid assistant' via searching：Avoice-WomenAid

or scanning the following QR code:


Please add remark about your country and/or region and name (nicknames also work)



The assistant will arrange a member from the administration team to help you, and will establish a small WeChat group of three people based on country and region. Women seeking help are able to fully communicate their situation and needs with the two volunteers in the WeChat group. The person responsible for this case will form a short-term and long-term assistance plan for each woman, and carry out communication, making notes, support, management and other work.

This is a work in progress. Stay tuned!
